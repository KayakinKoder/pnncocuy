Unfortunately there isn't much information out there about PNN El Cocuy, which is exactly why I spent a few days in Bogota after my trip to the park making this website. If you find anything better than what I have listed below, shoot me an email and I'll put a link up.

Official Colombia National Parks Website (in Spanish): http://www.parquesnacionales.gov.co/

If you can read Spanish (or use Google Translate), this website has a bit of information, and an amazing interactive topo map: http://www.nevados.org/en/sierra-nevada-del-cocuy.html

The always helpful Lonely Planet Thorn Tree Forum, searched for "cocuy." If you're going during off-season and worried about finding partners to do the 6 day round trip hike, post here: http://www.lonelyplanet.com/searchResult?q=cocuy
